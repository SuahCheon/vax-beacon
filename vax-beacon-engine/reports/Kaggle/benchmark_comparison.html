Key Findings

  • 62% concordance between Claude Sonnet 4.5 and MedGemma 4B on WHO AEFI classification — up from 54% in v3

  • MedGemma UC halved: 44→22 through original_case_text fix (prevents truncation from stripping clinical keywords) and numeric lab extraction (troponin values, LVEF%, CRP/BNP)
  • Nordic Study mechanistic signatures: 7-10 concordant cases detected, focal/diffuse LGE patterns captured — zero additional LLM calls (all keyword regex)
